22 June 2026

Feed the birds

Been following the RSPB's advice on summer feeding for a while now

more here.

essentially: pause feeding seeds and peanuts between May 1 and October 31 to prevent the spread of diseases like trichomonosis. I'm just providing fat balls (getting through two a day) and fresh water. Garden seems busier than ever (wonder if many have stopped feeding altogher in the summer) robins, blackbirds, sparrows, blue tits, woodpigeons and recently magpies! Blue tits and sparrows have vacated the boxes with their fledglings. Good stuff.
