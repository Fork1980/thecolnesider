22 June 2026

Feed the birds

Been following the RSPB's advice on summer feeding for a while now more here . Essentially: pause feeding seeds and peanuts between 1 May and 31 October to help prevent the spread of diseases like trichomonosis. I'm just providing fat balls and fresh water. The garden seems busier than ever. I wonder if many people have stopped feeding altogether in the summer. Robins, blackbirds, sparrows, blue tits, woodpigeons and recently magpies. Blue tits and sparrows have vacated the boxes with their fledglings. Good stuff.
